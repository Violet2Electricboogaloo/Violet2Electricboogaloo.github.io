What is copyright infringement?

Copyright infringement is the act of stealing or taking a legal property without doing one or more of the following (may change per company):

  • Obtaining permission from the company who owns it

  • Following the Fair Use laws

  • Abiding by the legal rights of the company who owns it

What makes it different than plagarism?

  • Plagarism, although a similar idea, rarely tends to lead to legal consequence.

  • Copyright infringement is based on law, and although punishments can very, it may lead to varying civil and criminal penalties.
